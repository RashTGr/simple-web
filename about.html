Introduction

This is an independent piece of coursework, and it requires students to create a simple website on a subject of their own preference. A student may NOT use any other code libraries from any other source in this assignment.

Design constraints:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Address Usability and Accessibility
  • Responsive Web Design (RWD)
  • Menu for internal navigation
  • Links to external websites
  • A section on the right side of page
  • At least a textual, video and audio description of the selected topic
  • Video/audio should not exceed more than 1 minute.

Module Info
